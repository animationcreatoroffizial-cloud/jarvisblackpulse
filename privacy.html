Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 10, 2026

BLACK PULSE ("we", "our", or "us") operates as an anime content creator on TikTok. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with our TikTok application and content.

1. Information We Collect

We do not directly collect personal data from viewers. When you interact with our TikTok content or application, TikTok may collect data as described in TikTok's own Privacy Policy. We only access data that TikTok makes available to us through their Developer API, which may include public profile information and content interaction data.

2. How We Use Information

Any data accessed through the TikTok API is used solely for the purpose of publishing and managing anime content on our TikTok account. We do not sell, share, or transfer this data to third parties.

3. Data Storage

We do not store personal data from TikTok users on our servers beyond what is technically required for content publishing operations. Any temporary data is deleted after processing.

4. Third-Party Services

Our content pipeline uses the following third-party services: TikTok (content platform), ElevenLabs (AI voice synthesis), and Anthropic Claude (AI story generation). These services have their own privacy policies which govern their data practices.

5. Your Rights

If you have questions about data related to your TikTok interactions with our content, please refer to TikTok's Privacy Policy and data request process. For any direct inquiries, contact us via TikTok.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the date at the top of this page.

7. Contact

For privacy-related questions, you can reach us through our TikTok profile.
